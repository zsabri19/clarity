The 5 Signs Diagnostic
01
Decision Latency
How long between decision and action? A gap exceeding 48 hours for items under $100K signals clarity is not installed. Speed reveals system design.
02
Role Ambiguity
Ask three people "Who owns X?" Three different answers = not an execution problem, but a clarity problem. Ownership must be singular and unambiguous.
03
Signal Overload
More than three priorities per person means nothing is a priority. Signal overload is the first symptom of a missing decision architecture.
04
Recurring Firefighting
The same crisis every quarter — same fire, same heroes — is not bad luck. It is a structural gap masked as urgency. Patterns repeat until the system changes.
05
Authority Confusion
When pressure hits, who decides? If the answer changes depending on who is in the room, governance is not real. Authority confusion is the #1 cause of transformation stalling.
Your next step
